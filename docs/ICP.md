# MooHive — Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary Persona

### AI Video Creator

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age Range | 22-45 |
| Location | Global, English-speaking markets (US, UK, EU, Australia) |
| Role/Title | Freelance video creator, motion designer, content creator, AI artist |
| Industry | Creative/Media, Marketing, Entertainment |
| Company Size | Solo creator or small studio (1-5 people) |

### Psychographics

**Goals:**
- Build recognition in the emerging AI video space
- Attract clients/collaborators who understand AI video's value
- Learn techniques from other creators to improve craft
- Stay current with rapidly evolving AI video tools

**Frustrations:**
- No dedicated platform to showcase AI video work (portfolio sites don't capture the process)
- Traditional video portfolios don't communicate the "how" (tools, prompts, techniques)
- Hard to find/connect with others doing similar work
- Clients don't know where to find AI video talent

**Motivations:**
- Being early to establish presence in a nascent field
- Community belonging with like-minded creators
- Learning/skill development through transparency
- Business development (getting hired)

### Behaviors

- **Tools they use:** AI video generators (Sora, Runway, Kling, Pika, Veo, Flow), editing software (Premiere, DaVinci, CapCut), image generators (Midjourney, DALL-E, Flux)
- **Where they hang out online:** Twitter/X, Discord (tool-specific servers), YouTube, Reddit (r/aivideo, r/StableDiffusion)
- **How they make decisions:** Peer recommendations, seeing others' results, tool capabilities, cost/value

## Secondary Persona

### Hirer/Appreciator

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age Range | 28-50 |
| Location | US, UK, EU (agency/brand hubs) |
| Role/Title | Creative director, marketing manager, agency producer, brand manager |
| Industry | Advertising, Marketing, Media production, Tech startups |
| Company Size | Mid-size to enterprise (50-5000+) |

### Psychographics

**Goals:**
- Find talented AI video creators for projects
- Understand what's possible with current AI video tech
- Stay ahead of creative trends

**Frustrations:**
- Don't know where to find vetted AI video talent
- Hard to assess creator capabilities without seeing process
- AI video quality varies wildly; need to filter for quality

**Motivations:**
- Deliver cutting-edge creative work for clients/brand
- Cost efficiency vs traditional video production
- Speed to market with AI-assisted content

### Behaviors

- **Tools they use:** Project management (Asana, Monday), communication (Slack, email), creative review (Frame.io)
- **Where they hang out online:** LinkedIn, industry publications, creative award sites
- **How they make decisions:** Portfolio quality, process transparency, responsiveness, referrals

## Jobs to Be Done

1. **When** I create an AI video I'm proud of, **I want to** share it with process details, **so I can** get recognition and attract opportunities.
2. **When** I'm learning AI video tools, **I want to** see what successful creators are doing, **so I can** improve my own techniques.
3. **When** I need to hire an AI video creator, **I want to** browse work by style/tool/capability, **so I can** find the right talent quickly.
4. **When** I discover a creator whose work I love, **I want to** easily contact them, **so I can** explore collaboration or hire them.

## Pain Points

| Pain Point | Severity (1-5) | Current Solution |
|------------|----------------|------------------|
| No dedicated showcase platform for AI video | 5 | Scattered across Twitter/X, YouTube, personal sites |
| Can't easily share creation process/metadata | 4 | Long captions, separate blog posts, Discord threads |
| Hard to discover creators by AI tool expertise | 5 | Word of mouth, manual searching |
| Hirers struggle to find/vet AI video talent | 4 | General freelance platforms, referrals |
| Difficult to learn techniques from top creators | 3 | YouTube tutorials, Discord communities |

## Value Proposition

### For AI video creators
### Who need a dedicated space to showcase work and share their creative process
### MooHive is a community portfolio platform
### That lets you display work with rich metadata (AI models, prompts, techniques)
### Unlike Behance, Dribbble, or social media
### We are purpose-built for AI video with process transparency and creator discovery

## Qualifying Questions

1. Do you create video content using AI tools (Sora, Runway, Kling, etc.)?
2. Are you looking to showcase your AI video work professionally?
3. Do you want to connect with other AI video creators or find talent to hire?
4. Are you interested in sharing/learning creation techniques and processes?

## Anti-Personas

- **Traditional video editors** uninterested in AI tools
- **Secret keepers** — creators who want to hide their techniques completely
- **One-time hobbyists** with no interest in community or feedback
- **Enterprise asset managers** — teams needing DAM functionality, not community
- **AI skeptics** — those opposed to AI-generated content on principle
